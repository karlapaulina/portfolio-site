Graphics

When Will was transitioning from working under a clinic to becoming his own business entity, he wanted to promote positivity and hope through his logo. Being that his first name carries a double meaning. With this in mind, the sun at it's back immediately came to mind.

A serif typeface was chosen to convey his professionalism, and his colour scheme was kept minimalist with a dark blue as an accent colour to compliment the white, open space.

Enviro Fireplaces launched their BluIQ control system in 2018, and requested a logo that conveyed it's environmentally-friendly energy saving benefits.

The colors chosen were inspired by natural elements, such as the sky and greenery. The sun and cloud icons and the reusable arrows further added to the efficient, eco-friendly message.
